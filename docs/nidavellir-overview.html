Why the verdict is trustworthy

①Crash oracle

A monitor sweeps every active arena and turns non-zero exits, OOM, crash-loops, panics and sanitizer aborts into deduped signals. A crash is scored evidence — even with no manifest.

②Perfect verification

A finding is confirmed only when proven: a reflected-XSS nonce reflected unescaped, a SQLi marker disclosed, an OAST callback, or crash correlation. Tri-state — unknown never fakes a pass.

③Structured Score

An Inspect-style verdict with a milestone Progress Rate that scores even a failed run, plus the full model + scaffold + cost tuple — so a number never stands alone.
